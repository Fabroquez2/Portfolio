Place your images in this folder to be used in the portfolio!

Image Filenames needed:
- profile.jpg    (Your profile picture used in the navbar and post headers)
- hero-bg.jpg    (The large background image for the first "Welcome" post)
- project-1.jpg  (Image for your first past project)
- project-2.jpg  (Image for your second past project)
- sponsor-ad.jpg (Optional image for the right sidebar ad)

Note: If the images are missing, the website will automatically load placeholder images from the internet so your layout doesn't break!
